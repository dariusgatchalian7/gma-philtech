The Humanities And Social Sciences (HUMSS) Strand Is One Of The Specialized Academic Tracks Available In Senior High School Curriculums. It Targets Students Who Have An Affinity For Subjects Related To Humanities, Social Sciences, Arts, Literature, And Culture.

ICT

The Information Communication And Technology (ICT) Strand Is Part Of The Technical-Vocational Livelihood (TVL) Track In The K-12 Curriculum. It Equips Students With Skills In Programming, Web Design, Animation, And Telecom Installation. Graduates Can Pursue Careers Such As Data Encoding, Programming, And Web Development. Choosing The ICT Strand Provides A Solid Foundation For IT-Related College Courses.
